## Notice of Annual General Body Meeting (Pothuyogam)

By the grace of God and with the blessings of His Grace the Patriarch, the parish of St. Mary's Malankara Syriac Orthodox Church, Oslo, held its **Annual General Body Meeting — Pothuyogam (പൊതുയോഗം)** for the year 2026.

## Date and Venue

**Date:** Saturday, 17th January 2026
**Time:** Following the Sthapana Perunnal Qurbana (approx. 12:30, after the fellowship lunch)
**Venue:** Eikeli Katolske Kirke – Kristi Freds Kirke, Veståsen 18, 1362 Oslo

## Sandhya Prarthana

An evening prayer (Sandhya Namaskaram) was held the previous evening — **Friday, 16th January 2026, at 19:00** — as part of the Perunnal preparations.

## Agenda

The Pothuyogam was conducted with the following agenda:

1. Opening Prayer
2. Welcome Address by the Vicar
3. Presentation of the Annual Report (2025–2026)
4. Presentation of the Annual Accounts
5. Selection of New Committee Members (2026–2027)
6. Sunday School and Family Unit Reports
7. Other Matters — Open Floor
8. Closing Prayer and Benediction

## Auction

An auction was held after the General Body Meeting, with generous contributions from parish members — handmade crafts, baked goods, and gift baskets. Proceeds supported parish activities for the coming year.

## A Note of Thanks

We thank every family who attended, contributed, and participated in the Pothuyogam. Your presence and commitment are what sustain the life of this parish.

May God bless our parish and guide the new committee through the coming year.
