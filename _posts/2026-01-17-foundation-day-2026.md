## Sthapana Perunnal Qurbana

Our 16th Parish Foundation Day was celebrated on Saturday, 17th January 2026, at 9:30 AM, followed by a fellowship brunch and lunch.

**Venue:** Eikeli Katolske Kirke - Kristi Freds Kirke, Veståsen 18, 1362 Oslo, Norway

## Sandhya Prarthana

An evening prayer (Sandhya Namaskaram) was held on Friday, 16th January 2026, at 19:00 as part of the Perunnal preparations.

## General Body Meeting

A General Body Meeting (Pothuyogam) was held after Qurbana with the following agenda:

1. Prayer
2. Welcome Address
3. Presentation of Annual Report
4. Presentation of Annual Accounts
5. Selection of New Committee Members
6. Other Matters
7. Closing Prayer and Benediction

## Auction

An auction was held after the General Body Meeting, with generous contributions from our parish members including handmade crafts, baked goods, and gift baskets.
