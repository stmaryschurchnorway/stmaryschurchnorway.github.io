Our church celebrated its 15th Foundation Day Perunnal on 23rd January 2025. The day began with Holy Qurbana led by Rev Fr. Eldhose Vattaparambil, followed by a community feast and cultural programme.

We thank all the families who participated and made this celebration memorable.
