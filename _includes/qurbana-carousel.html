Holy Qurbana

The Holy Qurbana is the central act of worship in the Malankara Syriac Orthodox Church, also known as the Eucharist or Divine Liturgy. It is a sacred commemoration of the Last Supper and Christ's redemptive work, celebrated with deep liturgical traditions spanning centuries of the Syriac Orthodox faith. The liturgy is divided into three major phases: the Preparation (performed behind the curtain), the Public Celebration (proclamation of Scripture), and the Anaphora (consecration and communion).
