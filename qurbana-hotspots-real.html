Words of Institution
സ്ഥാപന വാക്യങ്ങൾ (Sthapana Vakhyangal)
The priest pronounces the sacred words spoken by Christ at the Last Supper: "This is my Body" and "This is my Blood." These words effect the consecration of the bread and wine, transforming them into the mystical Body and Blood of Christ.
