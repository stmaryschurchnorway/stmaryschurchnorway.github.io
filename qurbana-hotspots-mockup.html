Qurbana Hotspots Feature

Click on the hotspots (golden circles) in the image below. When you hover, the hotspot highlights. Click it to scroll to the corresponding sacred item in the list below.

Example 1: Altar Items Stage
Stage Image

🍽️

Holds the consecrated bread representing the Body of Christ

hotspots:
  - item: "Chalice"
    x: 140
    y: 120
    radius: 40
  - item: "Paten"
    x: 275
    y: 135
    radius: 35

How it works:
  • Hover over the golden circles on the image to see item names
  • Click any hotspot to highlight and scroll to that sacred item below
  • Coordinates are defined in YAML as x, y (center point) and radius
  • Simple to maintain—no visual editor needed, just add coordinates to YAML
