Hotspot Design Options

Click on each option to see it in action. Hover over the hotspots to see the interaction. Vote for your favorite at the bottom!

Option 1: Thin Outline

Subtle 0.8px stroke with gentle pulse. Elegant tooltip on hover.

Option 2: Bold Glow

Filled circle with golden glow. Very noticeable and discoverable.

Option 3: Dot & Pulse

Small golden dot with expanding ring pulse. Compact and eye-catching.

Option 4: Gradient Spin

Rotating gradient fill. Modern and sophisticated animation.

Option 5: Concentric Rings

Multiple expanding rings. Strong visual draw with premium feel.

Option 6: Icon & Label

Text label with bouncing animation. Most explicit and direct.

Option 7: Minimal Dot

Tiny solid dot that grows on hover. Understated and clean.

✓ Pros: Modern and unique. Sophisticated animation. Stands out elegantly.
✗ Cons: Continuous rotation might be distracting. More CPU intensive animation.
