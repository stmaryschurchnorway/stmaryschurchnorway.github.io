================================================================================
HOLY MOORON USAGE — VERIFICATION
================================================================================

Question: When is Holy Mooron used - before or after immersion?

Answer: BOTH - Dual usage ✓

1. BEFORE IMMERSION:
   - Poured INTO the baptismal font in sign of cross
   - Stirred into water to sanctify it
   - Candidate immerses in Mooron-blessed water

2. AFTER IMMERSION:
   - Applied to candidate's body immediately after drying
   - Applied to organs of sense (eyes, ears, nostrils, lips)
   - Applied to entire body in cross-formation
   - This is the "Seal" (Hatma) - irrevocable marking

YOUR IMPLEMENTATION: ✓ CORRECTLY UNDERSTOOD
