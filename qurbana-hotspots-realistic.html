Preparation of Gifts
വിശുദ്ധ കുർബാന (Pronunciation)
The priest prepares the sacred gifts of bread and wine. This is a solemn moment of reverence as the elements are arranged upon the altar in preparation for the Eucharistic prayer.
Sacred Items Used
🏆
The Chalice (Kaso)
Holds the consecrated wine, representing the Blood of Christ shed for the redemption of mankind.
🍽️
The Paten (Pilaso)
Holds the consecrated bread, representing the Body of Christ given as an offering of salvation.
🍞
Sacred Bread (Lahmo)
Unleavened bread sanctified through the Eucharistic prayer, becoming the mystical Body of Christ.
🍷
Sacred Wine (Mazgogo)
Red wine sanctified through the Eucharistic prayer, becoming the mystical Blood of Christ.
⬜
Altar Stone (Tablitho)
Ancient stone symbolizing the table of the Last Supper and the unchanging foundation of the Church.

How This Works

✓ User Experience Flow:
  1. User sees image at top with golden circles showing clickable items
  2. User hovers to see what each hotspot is
  3. User clicks hotspot → that sacred item below highlights with a glow
  4. Carousel auto-scrolls to show the highlighted item while keeping image visible at top
  5. User can explore more items by clicking their hotspots
How to Add This to Your Stages:
- title: "Preparation of Gifts"
  image: "/path/to/image.jpg"
  hotspots:
    - item: "The Chalice (Kaso)"
      x: 120
      y: 100
      radius: 40
    - item: "The Paten (Pilaso)"
      x: 255
      y: 120
      radius: 35
Works because:
  • Image stays pinned at top—context is never lost
