# Holy Baptism (Mamodisa)

The Holy Baptism (Mamodisa) is the foundational sacrament through which individuals become members of Christ's mystical body and are incorporated into the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church. It is the sacrament of regeneration by water, the spiritual door through which believers enter into new life in Christ. In this sacrament, through the invocation of the Holy Spirit, water becomes the vehicle of divine grace—accomplishing the remission of all sin, original and actual, and transforming the candidate from a state of spiritual alienation into a child of God.
